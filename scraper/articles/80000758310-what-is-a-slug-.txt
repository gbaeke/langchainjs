A slug is a unique name that is used to identify your event. The slug will be included in the URL that leads to your event.Slug requirements: Keep it short and simpleNeeds to be unique Only lowercase charactersUse a dash to increase readability between multiple wordsExample of an event URL https://meet.thola.events/e/thola/demo-2021Thola = Organisation slugDemo-2021 = Event slugImportant: the organisation slug cannot be changed after adding your organisation to the dashboard. Just like your event slug cannot be changed after creating your event.
