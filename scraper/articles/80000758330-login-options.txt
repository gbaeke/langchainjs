There are 2 different options for your users to log in to your event: Log in with an account Log in with a one click loginLog in with an accountWhen your users will click on the login URL, they will be asked to:Log in with an account by setting a passwordLog in with Microsoft accountLog in with LinkedIn accountOnce they have completed the login, they will be automatically redirected to the event. Log in with a one click login A one click login is a unique URL per user which allows your attendees to automatically enter the event by clicking on the URL. This is the easiest way to log in given that your users don't need to sign in with an account. Read here how you can generate the login URL.
