It doesn't matter if you're organising an event for 20 or 200 people, in both scenarios it might be difficult to find your own avatar between the others. If you want your users to easily find their own avatar, you can configure a personal marker. Follow the next steps:Go to the dashboardSelect the event that you want to configureGo to general info & event settingsGo to profile configuration & custom cssGo to personal markerChoose which icon you want to useChoose the colors: Copy paste your hex-code in the text box Use the color picker symbol on the rightInsert the RGB-code when clicking on the color picker symbol on the right Don't forget to press 'save'
