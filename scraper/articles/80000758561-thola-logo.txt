In each room you will find the Thola logo displayed in the top left corner. By default our logo is shown in black and green. If our logo in white and green suits your room background better, then you can change it in the room settings in the visual editor.Follow the next steps to change the color of the Thola logo: Go to the dashboardSelect the event that you're configuringGo to rooms Select 'edit in visual editor' of the room in which you want to change the color of the Thola logoSelect 'room settings'Select 'general' Scroll down to 'Thola logo'Write 'white' in the text boxPress 'save' Important: if you have more rooms where you want the Thola logo to be white, then you need to configure this for each room via the room settings in the visual editor.
