A button is a variable element that can be used for different actions. Before configuring your button, you need to decide what content type you are going to use: image or icon with text. After that, you need to decide what type of button you need. There are four different button types:Link = website, video or any other embed URLMeeting = a meeting in Microsoft Teams or another communication platformBusiness card = a virtual business card that attendees can leave with the business card owner in a GDPR-proof wayAttendee Info Panel = the info card of an assigned attendee
