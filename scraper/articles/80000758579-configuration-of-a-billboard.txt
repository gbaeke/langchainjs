A billboard is one of the options that you can use to include sponsors or partners in your event. A billboard is a logo placed against the wall. If you click on this billboard more information will appear. More specifically, a billboard is a group element that consists out of a pop-over image, three buttons and an embedded element.The structure of a billboard in fixed, namely the pop-over image and buttons on the left, whereas the embedded element is placed on the right. However, you are free to chose which of these elements you want to incorporate into your billboard. How to configure a billboard Go to the dashboardSelect the event that you want to configureGo to 'rooms'Click on the 'visual editor icon' of the room in which you want to add a billboardClick on 'add element' and select 'billboard'Go to the properties on the left sideGive your billboard a name (optional)Choose the location and size of your billboardUse your computer mouseUse your keyboard commandsFill in the x and y-axis, width and heightSelect 'Always behind' if you want this billboard to always be placed behind other objects or avatars Upload your billboard by selecting an image from the media library (mostly a logo)Select 'Flip horizontally' if you want to mirror the image over the x-axisFill in the elements that you want to add to your billboardAbout us text = the text that will be displayed on the button that leads to your (or your partner's) websiteAbout us URL = insert the website herePop-over image = this is another image that you can include in your billboard Embedded URL = insert a video, postcast, subscription form or any other embedded URL hereMeeting slug = insert a meeting button in your billboard by filling in the slug of your meetingBusinesss card slug = insert a business card button in your billboard by filling in the slug of your business card Press 'save'Important: don't forget to assign the role of meeting owner or businesss card collector if you want somebody (or multiple people) to manage this meeting or to collect the business cards that were left.
