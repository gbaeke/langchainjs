Doors are used to navigate from one room to another. Doors can be visualised by an actual door or you can choose any other image if you rather want to use a more secret entrance. For example you can choose a plant as a secret door.When configuring doors it's very important that you indicate to which room this door needs to lead. How to configure a doorGo to the dashboardSelect the event that you want to configureGo to 'rooms'Click on the 'visual editor icon' of the room in which you want to add a doorClick on 'add element' and select 'door'Go to the properties on the left sideGive your door a name (required)Choose the location and size of your doorUse your computer mouseUse your keyboard commandsFill in the x and y-axis, width and heightSelect 'always behind' if you want this door to always be placed behind other objects or avatars Upload your door by selecting an image from the media library Select 'flip horizontally' if you want to mirror the image over the x-axisChoose to which room your door needs to lead by selecting the slug of that room from the dropdown menuImportant: always make sure that the room to which your door needs to lead, has already been createdIndicate if you'd like to have a maximum capacity of guests for this room (optional)Add a name plate to your doorSelect 'add name plate'Fill in the name of your door under 'text'Choose the font and background color Copy paste your hex-code in the text box Use the color picker symbol on the rightInsert the RGB-code when clicking on the color picker symbol on the right Select the tick box if you want to show shadow around the name plate of your door Important: you can use 'back to door' under 'properties' to return to the properties of your door. Press 'save'
