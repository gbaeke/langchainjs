A big screen is recommended when you have multiple sessions that will occur after each other. You can integrate pre-recorded content, a live stream or a mix of both. With a big screen you can also display a schedule. How to configure a big screenGo to the dashboardSelect the event that you want to configureGo to 'rooms'Click on the 'visual editor icon' of the room in which you want to add a big screenClick on 'add element' and select 'big screen'Go to the properties on the left sideGive your big screen a name (optional)Choose the location and size of your big screenUse your computer mouseUse your keyboard commandsFill in the x and y-axis, width and heightSelect 'always behind' if you want this big screen to always be placed behind other objects or avatars Select 'hide on mobile' if you don't want this big screen to be visible on the mobile client. Upload your big screen by selecting an image from the media librarySelect 'flip horizontally' if you want to mirror the image over the x-axisSelect 'hide styling' if you want hide the styling of the big screen component and only use the styling of the session. The white frame won't be displayed then: Add your sessionsGive your session a name (optional)Choose the location and size of your session (see options above)Select an image from the media library for your session (for example: Teams logo)Select your start and end time of your sessionTick the box 'show embedded content in the session' if applicableGive your session a 'title' and 'description'Add or select a slug under 'meeting slug'Add another session if applicable (the easiest way is to duplicate your first session and then edit it)Add a schedule if wantedChoose the location and size of your session (see options above)Give your schedule a titlePress 'save' Important: you can use 'back to big screen' under 'properties' to return to your big screen and to continue adding new sessions to it.
