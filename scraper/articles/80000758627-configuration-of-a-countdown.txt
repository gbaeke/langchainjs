A countdown element can be used to indicate when a session will start. You can also use it to guide your users to a certain room.How to configure a countdownGo to the dashboardSelect the event that you want to configureGo to 'rooms'Click on the 'visual editor icon' of the room in which you want to add a countdownClick on 'add element' and select 'countdown'Go to the properties on the left sideGive your countdown a name (optional)Choose the location and size of your countdownUse your computer mouseUse your keyboard commandsFill in the x and y-axis, width and heightSelect 'always behind' if you want this countdown to always be placed behind other objects or avatars Select the 'background and border color' of your countdownCopy paste your hex-code in the text box Use the color picker symbol on the rightInsert the RGB-code when clicking on the color picker symbol on the right Select the 'font size'You can choose between pixel (px), root element (rem) and view width (vw)Add a targetInsert the 'title' of your countdownSet your 'target time and date'Press 'save'Extra: you can add multiple targets, which will automatically appear after the other one is finished.
