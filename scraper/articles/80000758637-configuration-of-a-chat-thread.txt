A chat thread is a white page on which your attendees can leave messages. These messages will be visible for everyone in the room. As an organisation owner or event manager you can delete and download the chat history via the dashboard. How to configure a chat threadGo to the dashboardSelect the event that you want to configureGo to 'rooms'Click on the 'visual editor icon' of the room in which you want to add a chat threadClick on 'add element' and select 'chatthread'Go to the properties on the left sideGive your chatthread a name (optional)Choose the location and size of your chat threadUse your computer mouseUse your keyboard commandsFill in the x and y-axis, width and heightSelect 'always behind' if you want the chat thread to always be placed behind other objects or avatars Insert a 'title'Insert a 'background and accent (border) color'Copy paste your hex-code in the text box Use the color picker symbol on the rightInsert the RGB-code when clicking on the color picker symbol on the right Press 'save'How to delete the chat historyGo to the dashboardSelect the event that you want to configureGo to 'rooms'Click on the 'visual editor icon' of the room where you want to delete the chat historySelect your chat threadGo to the properties on the left sideSelect 'clear chat history'Press 'save'How to download the chat historyGo to the dashboardSelect the event that you want to configureGo to 'rooms'Click on the 'visual editor icon' of the room where you want to download the chat historySelect your chat threadGo to the properties on the left sideSelect 'download chat history'An excel file will be downloaded
