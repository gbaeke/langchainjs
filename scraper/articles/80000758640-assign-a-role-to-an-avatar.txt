If you want to give a certain attendee a fixed location among the other attendees, you can assign a role to this avatar. This can be convenient if you want one of your speakers or your CEO to stand out more.Follow the next steps:Go to the dashboardSelect the event that you want to configureGo to 'rooms'Click on the 'visual editor icon' of the room in which you want to edit your avatar positionsSelect 'locations' under 'avatar display style' (below in the viusal editor) Select one of the avatars Choose the location and size of this avatarSelect a role on the left side Press 'save'Important: if you assign roles to avatars, then you have to make sure that these persons have been assigned the right roles via your user management. Important: if you want this person to have a specific avatar, then you first need to upload an avatar and then assign this avatar to this person.
