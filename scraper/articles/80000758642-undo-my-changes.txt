In case that you have already saved your room after applying some changes, but rather want to you have your previous version back, then you can restore this via 'view change history':Go to the dashboardSelect the event that you're configuringGo to 'rooms'Select the 'visual editor icon' of the room that you want to editSelect 'view change history'Here you will get an overview of all the changes that have been made to this room and by whom.The version on top of the list is the most recent one.Select the version that you would like to restoreSelect 'load this version'
