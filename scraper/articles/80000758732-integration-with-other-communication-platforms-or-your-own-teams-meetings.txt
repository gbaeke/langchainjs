If you rather want to use other communication platforms such as Google Meet or Zoom for your pre-plannend meetings,  when you organise a Microsoft Teams Live Event or when you want to add your own MS Teams URL's , then you need to add a custom URL to your original meeting. Follow the next steps:Go to the dashboardSelect the event that you want to configureGo to 'meetings'Check whether you can find the meeting that you want to adapt in the meeting listIf not: select 'add meeting'Open the meeting details by clicking on the meetingGo to 'custom meeting URL'Enable this option by sliding the button to the rightEnter the URL of your meeting in the field belowPress 'save'!! Important: in case you're using a MS Teams URL, please check that you insert the right URL. Once your meeting has been created, you can open the meeting appointment in your calendar.
