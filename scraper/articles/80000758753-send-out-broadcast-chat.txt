Another option that you can use to inform your attendees, is by sending out a "broadcast chat. This message can be send out by Thola staff, organisation owners or event managers and it will appear like a chat message. Follow the next steps to send out a broadcast chat:Go to the dashboardSelect the event in which you want to send out a broadcast chatGo to announcementsSelect 'broadcast chat'Type in your messageSelect high priority if you want to display it as a pop-up message in the middle of the screen of your users
