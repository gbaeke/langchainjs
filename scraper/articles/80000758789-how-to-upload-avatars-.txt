How to upload avatarsYou can choose to use other avatars that are available in the media library under 'avatars' or you can upload your own avatars. If you want to use your own ones, you first need to upload them in the media library. Then you need to decide to which level you want to upload them: 'organisation' or 'event' level.  There are two options to upload avatars:ManuallyIn case you only have one avatar that you need to add, you can do it manually by clicking on 'add avatar'. Then the media library will open and you can select your preferred avatar. In bulkIf you have multiple avatars that you want to upload, its easier to work with a bulk upload. Select 'bulk avatar creation' and then choose which avatars you want to upload from your media library.
