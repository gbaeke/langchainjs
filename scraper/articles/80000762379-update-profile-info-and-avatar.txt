Even after uploading your users, you can still update their profile information and avatar. Go to the dashboardSelect the right eventGo to 'users'Click on the user that you want to editIn the user details you can:Update the information in the profile fields (for example: first name, last name, job title or organisation)Change the avatarUpload a profile picture Assign platform and event rolesDon't forget to press 'save'Important: the e-mail cannot be changed. If your user wants to log in with a different e-mail, you will have to add this person as a new user.
