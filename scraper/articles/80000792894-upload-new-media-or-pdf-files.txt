Next to using our standard assets, you can also upload your own media files.How to upload new filesFollow the next steps: Go to the dashboardSelect the event for which you want to add new mediaGo to the media librarySelect 'upload media'Choose to which level you want to add this media Select the file(s) you want to uploadSelect 'upload media'Important: the filename of your media cannot be changed once it has been uploadedAllowed files in media libraryOur media library allows images (PNG, JPEG and GIF files) and PDF files with a maximum size of 2 megabytes.If you want to make your image smaller in size, you can use: https://imagecompressor.com/
