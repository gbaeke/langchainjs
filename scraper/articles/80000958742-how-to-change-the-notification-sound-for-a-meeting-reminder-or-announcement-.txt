By default we use a common 'ping' sound effect to display a meeting reminder or an announcement. In case you want to use a custom sound, you can set this up as following:How to configure a custom sound for a meeting reminder or announement: Prepare your media file in the following format: File: only WAV files are supportedSize: max. 1 MB Name: save your media file(s) with the following name on your pc/laptop: meeting-reminder-XX can be text and numbers.For example: meeting-reminder-01 OR meeting-reminder-startImportant: If you don't save it with the correct name, your custom sound won't work. Go to the dashboardSelect the event for which you want to schedule a meeting or announcementGo to the media libraryUpload your media file(s)Important: when you have multiple custom sounds, they will be played randomly
