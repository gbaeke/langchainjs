Once you are done configuring the bulk avatar positions for one room and you would like to copy them into another room, you can now do so via the visual editor. Once you have copied them, you can easily paste the avatar position into the other rooms.How to copy avatar positionsGo to the dashboardOpen the event in which you want to copy avatar positionsGo to 'rooms' in the menuOpen the visual editor of the room of which you want to copy the avatar positionsSelect 'room settings'Select 'avatar positions'Select 'copy all avatar positions' on the left sideOpen the the room where you want to paste the avatar position in the visual editorSelect 'room settings'Select 'avatar positions'Select 'paste avatar positions' on the left sidePress 'save'You can paste as many times as you like.
