A test event is recommended to experiment with the configuration of all the Thola features before setting up your real event. Keep in mind the following: Each organisation is entitled to one test event. You cannot have multiple test eventsYour test event can be open until the expiration date of your license. It's not limited to 7 days as with regular events.You can invite up to 15 people in this eventImportant: you will always see a notifation in the top right corner to remind you that you are in a test event. Therefore it's not recommended to invite customers or external stakeholders to your test event.
